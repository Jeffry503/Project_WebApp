Our Services

Our Services

Design Consulation

We communicate with the designer to discuss the problems you are having with your space. The designer will walk through your room(s) to get a real sense of the problems, ask you questions on how you use your space and will share their ideas on how best to proceed.

Partial Service

We get our designer to help you with a specific task in a specific amount of time. For example, you might need help just picking out some tile for your bathroom so you meet the designer at a tile store and that’s it. It’s perfect for getting help with smaller projects.

Full Service

From your beginning meeting to your final reveal, the interior designer is doing everything for you. The designer will help you step-by-step in creating the space or home of your dreams. After getting a sense of your personal unique style, they will get to work to create the design plan, communicate plans to the contractor and tradespeople and take care of every little detail.
